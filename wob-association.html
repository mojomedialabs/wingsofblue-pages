Wings of Blue Association Mission Statement

The Wings of Blue Association (WOBA) is an independent, non-profit, civilian organization dedicated to supporting the Wings of Blue - the U.S. Air Force Skydiving Team, and itâ€™s interests. WOBA also promotes the Wings of Blue mission by increasing public awareness within the Air Force and local communities.

WOBA was founded in 2010 by Glenn Paluch, Lt. Col Matt Rytting and USAFA Cadet Second Class Ryan Fantasia. WOBA is an all-volunteer organization with virtually no overhead. All donations and revenue go directly to support team.

The Wings of Blue Association is an IRS Code 501(c)(3) organization. Contributions to WOBA are fully tax deductible for federal income tax purposes.
