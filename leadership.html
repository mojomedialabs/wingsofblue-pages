• Lt Col Mike Love

    • Hometown:Grand Prairie, TX
    • Squadron Position:Commander
    • Career Highlights:Casual Lt in the 94 ATS (when there was just a jump flight); flying the B-1B, T-38, and F-15C; returning to the 98 FTS as Ops Officer and then Commander
    • Biggest Motivation Behind Working at the 98th FTS:It’s been such a blessing to return to the team that meant so much to me as a cadet and getting a chance to try and make things better for current/future cadets. There is no better cadet leadership laboratory than our parachuting programs and I’m glad to be part of it again.
    • Favorite Jump:My first AFF-I jump with C2C Zach Mills and jumping into Mile High Stadium with my 4-way team as a cadet.
    • Why you enjoy jumping:It really is “flight in its purest form”…stand in the door!
    • Outside Jumping (What you Do):Spend as much time as I can with my family: wife Mauri, sons Brody and Braden, and daughter Brenna.

  • Capt Sarah Towler
